Under Construction

Overview

Problem

Brainstorming with LLMs to improve results is promising, but typical outputs skew safe, vague, or generic.

Challenges

  • LLMs often fall back on tried-and-true ideas.
  • Giving suggestions “with personality” and specificity is difficult.
  • LLMs require structure to weed out ideas and develop only the winners.

Solution

  • Use temperature setting effectively
  • Explore diverse prompts: stating the problem from different perspectives
  • Structuring the conversation between LLM agents to combine and develop good ideas
  • Weed out not-promising ideas
  • Develop the winners into full design brief documents for user review.
