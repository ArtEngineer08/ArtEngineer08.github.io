Endless Sky Trader’s Starmap

In-world navigation and commerce HUD for a roaming merchant: plot routes, gauge fuel, and sniff out profit while dodging trouble.

Overview

This mockup imagines the starmap a veteran trader would actually use aboard ship in the game Endless Sky.

It emphasizes legibility at-a-glance during flight while revealing deeper trade and risk intel on demand. This mock-up shows how all the details in the game's map could be themed as an in-world UI for the captain.

How it works

  • Port Details: Click on a star system to see the details of that system's ports (randomly-generated for this mock-up).
  • Trade Signals: Inline badges summarize buy/sell deltas from your last port intel; todo: expand to see commodity spreads and cargo fit.
  • Risk & Territory Layers: See the badge of the faction that controls the system, pirate activity, and security.
  • System Drawer: Side panel includes local services (outfitter/shipyard/bank), hazards, and asteroids.
